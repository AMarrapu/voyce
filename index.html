Features
Everything you need to speak with power
14 AI-powered metrics covering both your voice and your presence.
🎙️ 9 Voice Metrics
🗣️

Speaking Rate

Tracks WPM and guides you toward the optimal 120–150 range.

⏸️

Pause Control

Identifies strategic vs unplanned pauses. Teaches you to use silence as a tool.

📢

Volume & Energy

Measures vocal intensity variation to ensure your delivery commands attention.

🚫

Filler Word Detection

Catches every "um," "uh," "like," and "you know" — tracks rate per minute over time.

🎵

Pitch & Intonation

Analyzes pitch variation to identify monotone delivery.

💎

Voice Stability

Measures jitter and shimmer — the acoustic markers of nervousness.

⚡

Articulation Rate

Counts syllables per second during active speech.

📚

Vocabulary Complexity

Analyzes lexical diversity and word choice.

😊

Speech Fluency

Detects hesitations and repetitions — a composite confidence score.

📹 5 Presence Metrics
👁️

Eye Contact

Tracks gaze direction and consistency — a powerful signal of confidence.

🧍

Posture

Analyzes body alignment and how your stance projects authority.

🚶

Movement

Detects purposeful vs nervous movement patterns.

🤲

Hand Gestures

Measures gesture frequency and naturalness.

😄

Facial Expression

Detects expressiveness and emotional range.
